Projects

Course Management System

The Course Management Tool allows users to copy one or more courses simultaneously into the District Domain. Courses are limited to a single subscription. Pearson does this to ensure our customers copy down our national or state courses. Filtering the available content by subscription takes the guess work out of it. Courses can also be located by using a text search, if desired. After selecting the filters, select  List Courses.

Technologies: NodeJs, React, MongoDB, Docker, Jenkins, Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Learning Management Syatem(LMS)

The Pearson Learning Management System (LMS) is a platform developed by Pearson Education to enhance online learning and teaching experiences. It integrates a suite of tools, content, and resources designed to support both educators and students. Pearson's LMS solutions are often tailored to specific learning contexts, whether for K-12 education, higher education, or professional training.

Technologies: Java 17, Spring Boot, Microservices, Docker, Jenkins, Amazon Web Services (AWS), RESTful Web Services

A Mobile-Based Screening and Refinement System to Identify the Risk of Dyscalculia and Dysgraphia Learning Disabilities in Primary School Students - Microsoft Imagine Cup World Championship 2022 Runners-up

  • Chamil Diluksha
  • Mobile Application

Project "Nana Shilpa" is the first mobile application designed to identify and address Learning Disability (LD) risks in primary school students providing necessary guidance for improvement using native language. This application contains a set of questionnaires for identifying process and remediation. This has been recognized as an acceptable solution for LD screening and intervention in Sri Lanka. 4 team members have given their contribution to this project which have being won nation and international level awards. I have given my contribution as the team lead and the lead developer.

Education is the most powerful thing in the world. Every child around the world has equal rights to it. Since these Learning Disabilities (LDs) cause direct effect on the brain there is no cure. But with the proper guidance with expert-approved activities, the situation can be improved a lot.

Due to the lack of awareness the students with LDs may categorize weak compared with their fellow peers. The main goal of this project is to identify whether there is a possible risk and if it is, provide remedy activities. A parent or a teacher can track progress since all the done activity results are being saved. We have provided this application for the students in 10 schools. We are still receiving very positive feedback from them. That's the most important achievement we have gained so far. We intend to launch this application globally to ensure that everyone can access it whenever assistance is required. This will come with a variety of LD variations, increasing its usability for a wider range of age groups and making it suitable for multiple native languages. The objective of this application is to provide essential support to students who may be at risk of learning disabilities or those who will be born with such conditions. Addressing the issue of providing adequate education for children is essential as they represent the future of the world. Project "Nana Shilpa" stands out among other solutions due to its comprehensive coverage of various LDs, attractive scenery, sound guidance, alignment with school curriculum, and developed under expert guidance.

Additionally, this was presented at the IEEE 10th International Conference on Information and Automation for Sustainability (ICIAfS) in 2021 and published as a research article. Both national and international awards have been given to this project. The National ICT Awards - NBQSA 2021 merit award is one of them. And as the major win, over 160 countries participating in the global software competition known as Microsoft Imagine Cup, the well-known Microsoft Corp. has shown the importance of this application. It gives me great pleasure to announce that we participated and finished in second place in person at the Microsoft Headquarters in Seattle, Washington in 2022.
